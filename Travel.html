Turkey

My travel experience in Turkey was an enchanting adventure filled with rich cultural discoveries and breathtaking sights. From exploring the historical marvels of Istanbul, such as the majestic Hagia Sophia and the vibrant Grand Bazaar, to marveling at the stunning landscapes of Cappadocia and the turquoise waters of Pamukkale, every moment was awe-inspiring. The warmth and hospitality of the Turkish people, coupled with the delectable cuisine and immersive cultural experiences, made my journey an unforgettable one.

Greece

My family vacation in Greece was an absolute dream come true. From the iconic historical sites of Athens, like the majestic Acropolis and the ancient ruins of Delphi, to the idyllic islands of Santorini and Mykonos with their picturesque white-washed buildings and stunning sunsets, every moment was filled with beauty and wonder.

Germany

My travel experience in Germany with the Erasmus+ project was an incredible blend of cultural immersion, learning, and friendship. From the moment I arrived, I was warmly welcomed by the German hosts and fellow participants from different countries.
